HAPPY BIRTHDAY

Dear Friend,

To My Dear Friend
On your special day,
A celebration in every way.
The joy you bring, the light you share,
Makes life more bright, beyond compare.

With laughter and warmth, your heart so true,
A gift to all who cherish you.
Through highs and lows, you always stay,
A constant light, a steady ray.

So here's to you, on this day so fine,
May your path be filled with joy divine.
Happy birthday, my treasured friend,
May our bond grow strong, never end.

And at last,
Happy Birthday To You, Rio.

- By Your Friend [Well-Wisher]
