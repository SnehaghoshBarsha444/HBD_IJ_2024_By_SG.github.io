HAPPY BIRTHDAY

Dear Bestie,

For You, On Your 19th Birthday
Nineteen candles, each one bright,
For a friend who’s always been my light.
Though words aren’t spoken every day,
In every silence, you find a way.
We don’t need to talk for hours long,
To know our friendship’s always strong.
You lift me up when I feel low,
Your quiet love helps me to grow.
We’ve guided each other, through wrong and right,
And in dark times, you’ve been my light.
A smile, a word, a gentle hand,
You understand when no one can.
Nineteen years, and here you stand,
A shining soul, with a heart so grand.
Though we don’t always speak aloud,
You’ve made me grateful, you’ve made me proud.
So today I wish you joy and more,
May life’s blessings come ashore.
For you, my friend, are truly rare,
I’ll always be here, I’ll always care.
So here’s to you, my kindred friend, my sister through the years,
Let’s raise a glass to memories, to laughter, and to tears.
Though paths may twist and wander, one truth remains the same:
Our friendship shines eternal, like an everlasting flame.

- By Your Dearest Companion
