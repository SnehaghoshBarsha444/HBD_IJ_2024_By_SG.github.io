HAPPY BIRTHDAY

Dear Elder Brother,

To My Beloved Brother


In you, I find the strength to boldly soar,
A guiding light through life’s uncertain maze,
Your wisdom leads me through the stormy haze,
And opens paths I could not see before.


You are the friend whose heart I do adore,
Who lifts me up with love in countless ways,
A mentor true, whose kindness lights my days,
And shows me dreams worth chasing evermore.


Your presence is my anchor, calm and sure,
With every step, I know I walk with grace,
For in your eyes, I see the light of home.


On this, your day, may joy in you endure,
For you, dear brother, time cannot erase—
The one I cherish most, where'er I roam.


- By Your Dearest Sister
