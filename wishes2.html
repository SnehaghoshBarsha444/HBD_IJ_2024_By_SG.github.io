HAPPY BIRTHDAY

Dear Friend,

To My Friend


Across the miles, from hill to sea,
A friendship bloomed so naturally.
From the North-East winds to Southern skies,
We share our hearts through words and ties.

Though we’ve never met face to face,
Your kindness fills this virtual space.
Through chats and laughs, both big and small,
You’ve been a friend, the truest of all.

On your birthday, I send this cheer,
To you, my friend, so far, yet near.
May joy and light fill up your day,
And all your dreams find their own way.

From hills to sea-shores,
This bond is strong, forevermore.
Though distance parts us, it’s no wall,
Our friendship stands, and that’s all!


- By Your Friend [Well-Wisher]
